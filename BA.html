BLUE ARCHIVE

The player and protagonist is a Sensei who was summoned from outside the academic city-state of Kivotos by the president of the General Student Council, an extrajudicial committee governing the schools. Following her sudden disappearance, criminal activity rises around Kivotos and the player is tasked by the remaining members of the council to resolve issues that crop up and help search for the president.

STUDENTS

Fitting of its name as the Academy City, a majority of the inhabitants of Kivotos are students. They live alongside human-like animals and robots who are treated similarly to humans. Kivotos is mostly run by its students, including the General Student Council which oversees all of Kivotos. Likewise, most schools are self-managed by the students of that specific school, typically in the form of their student councils.

KIVOTOS

Kivotos was made from the union of thousands of academies. The academies are divided into mostly independent districts. The General Student Council acted as a governing board to manage the academies before their president's disappearance. After that, the city became an anarchist society.
